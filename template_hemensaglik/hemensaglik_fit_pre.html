You frequently have pains or pressure in the left or mid-chest area, left side of the neck, shoulder, or arm - during or right after exercise.
You often feel faint or have severe dizzy spells.
You experience extreme breathlessness after mild exertion.

You have bone or joint problems such as arthritis.
You are over age 60 and not accustomed to vigorous exercise.
Your father, mother, brother or sister had a heart attack before age 50.
